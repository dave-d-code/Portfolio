www.Locumhire.co.uk

Complex Web App to allow Hospital HR to auto hire medical staff on behalf of an Agency.
(Under development.. reaching final stages)

Coding Attributes:

  • Written in the PHP Codeigniter framework.
  • Also uses Bootstrap, Jquery, Ajax.
  • Secure site, which gives different UI's to 3 types of end user:
    • Hospital HR staff.
    • Agency Admins.
    • Freelance Medical staff.
  • Database driven algorithms to auto notify the correct medical staff re hospital requests for hiring staff for a shift.
  • Freelance medical staff can self book jobs having been selected by the site. Agency and Hospital are updated of progress.
  • Agency Admin section can overview bookings with minimum interaction needed.
  • Ajax alert features to auto notify Agency Admins of hospital requests.
  • Site monitors and hightlights "problematic" bookings with Agency UI features to deal with them.
